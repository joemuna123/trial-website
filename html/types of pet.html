1. Mammal Pets

Mammal Pets are companion animals belonging to the class Mammalia, which are kept primarily for companionship or entertainment. These pets are characterized by their ability to produce milk for their young, having fur or hair, and being warm-blooded vertebrates.

2.Bird Pets

Bird pets, or pet birds, are birds that people keep as companions. They come in various species, sizes, and colors, and they often form strong bonds with their owners. Some popular pet bird species include parrots (like budgies, cockatiels, and African greys), canaries, finches, and lovebirds.

3.Fish Pets

Fish pets, or pet fish, are aquatic animals that people keep in tanks or aquariums for companionship and decoration. They come in a dazzling variety of species, sizes, colors, and patterns, making them a popular choice for pet owners. Some common pet fish include goldfish, betta fish, guppies, angelfish, and tetras
